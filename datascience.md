1. what is data science ?

Data Science is the process of collecting, cleaning, analyzing, and interpreting data to find useful insights. It combines programming, statistics, and machine learning to help organizations make better decisions.

2\) Where is it Used?

Data Science is used in healthcare, banking, e-commerce, social media, education, and transportation. Companies use it for customer analysis, fraud detection, recommendation systems, and business forecasting.

3\) Required Skill Sets

Python

SQL

Statistics \& Probability

Machine Learning

Data Visualization (Power BI, Tableau)

Excel

Basic Cloud Knowledge (AWS)

4\) Career Opportunities

Data Science offers roles such as Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, Business Analyst, and AI Engineer. These roles are in high demand across many industries.

5\) Future Scope

The demand for Data Science professionals is expected to grow rapidly due to increasing data generation and AI adoption. It is considered one of the most promising and high-paying technology careers.
